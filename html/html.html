computer programmer, sometimes called only programmer or more recently a coder (especially in more informal contexts), is a person who creates computer software. A programmer's most oft-used computer language (e.g., Assembly, COBOL, C, C++, C#, JavaScript, Lisp, Python) may be prefixed to the term programmer. Some who work with web programming languages also prefix their titles with web.
